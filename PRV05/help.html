Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Please check your entire booking very carefully before completing your transaction. We do not recieve payments or give refunds on online.
You can make changes to your bookings at any time, up to 24 hours before the date and time of your first journey. An amendment fee and
booking fee will apply for each amended reservation. Changes can be made online or with an authorised otr agent. The amendment fee at
an authorised otr agent may be different to the amendment fee charged online.
If your new journey is more expensive than the original one, you will also need to pay the difference between the two fares.

To change your booking, email us at: teamb@otr.com

We recommend to book early to avoid disappointment. Only seats shown on the website as reserved seats may be reserved.
All other seats are on a first come first serve basis.

Prices will vary by reservation date, day and time of travel, time of booking, and demand for seats.
Prices are subject to change without notice.

After filling out your trip details, and selecting your desired route, you'll be able to choose from available reserved seating.
Simply click on the seat you'd like, click "continue", and purchase your tickets. Reserved seats are first come, first served, and
available on selected routes.

Your ticket will show the seat number you have reserved. On the coach a headrest with a number will identify your seat.

Reserved seats are currently available on select routes. If your trip offers seat reservations, you will be able to select a specific seat at
the time of purchase.

While the seat may be available when leaving your departure point, it may be reserved by another passenger from a city further along your trip.
Please respect these arrangements and refrain from moving to a reserved seat.

If you cannot find the answer to your question please email us at: teamb@otr.com and we will endeavour to respond to
your email within 72 hours. If your query is about an existing otr booking, please remember to supply your booking number and/or
ticket reservation number.

Due to the Coronavirus pandemic, we are working differently to best support our staff and customers. If you have any other enquires please contact us here.
